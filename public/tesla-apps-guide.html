üí° Pro Tips for Tesla Browsing

  • Use Parked Mode: Ensure your Tesla is in Park for full browser functionality
  • Connect to Wi-Fi: Use Wi-Fi when available to save cellular data
  • Bookmark Favorites: Create bookmarks for frequently used apps
  • Enable Full Screen: Use full screen mode for better app experience
  • Voice Commands: Use voice commands to navigate without touching the screen
  • Multiple Tabs: Keep multiple apps open in different tabs
  • Clear Cache: Regularly clear browser cache for optimal performance
