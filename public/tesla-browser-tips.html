Tesla Browser Tips & Tricks

Unlock the full potential of your Tesla's web browser with these helpful tips, designed to work perfectly with TeslaLink.

Bookmark for Instant Access

The most important tip: bookmark TeslaLink! When you have the site open, tap the star icon or "Add to Favorites" in the browser's address bar. This makes launching your dashboard as simple as opening the browser and tapping a single button.

Mastering Fullscreen Video

The Tesla browser doesn't have a native fullscreen button, but TeslaLink provides a solution. Use the "Fullscreen" button on the dashboard to create a synchronized, address bar-free viewing window. Launch a video app, and it will play in that clean, immersive view. This is perfect for YouTube, Netflix, and other streaming services.

Use the QR Scanner for Easy App Adding

Typing long URLs in the car is a hassle. Instead, use TeslaLink's "Scan QR" feature. Find the website you want on your phone, use your phone's share feature to create a QR code for the URL, and then scan it with your Tesla's screen. It's the fastest way to build your custom dashboard.

Keep Your Driver Profile

Your TeslaLink dashboard is saved in the browser's local storage, which is tied to your Tesla's driver profile. If you share your car, your custom app layout will be there when you return to your profile.

Clearing Browser Data

If you ever experience issues with TeslaLink or another website, try clearing your browser's data. You can do this in the car's settings. Warning: This will erase all saved data, including your custom TeslaLink dashboard, so use it as a last resort.
