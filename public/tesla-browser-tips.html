Keep it Fast & Clean

For optimal speed, regularly clear your browser's cache to prevent it from getting bloated. You can do this in the browser's settings. Also, while multiple tabs are useful, try to keep only a few essential ones open to conserve resources.

Master Navigation

Use touch gestures like pinch-to-zoom and two-finger scrolling. Organize your favorite sites with bookmarks for quick access, and use voice commands like "Navigate to..." for hands-free browsing when it's safe to do so.

Stay Connected

Whenever possible, connect to a trusted Wi-Fi network (like at home or a Supercharger) instead of relying on cellular data. Wi-Fi is often faster and more stable, providing a better streaming and browsing experience.

Browse Privately

Use the browser's private or incognito mode when handling sensitive information like banking, or if you're using a shared Tesla. This prevents your history and data from being saved on the vehicle.

Block Annoyances

For a cleaner and safer experience, enable the pop-up blocker in your browser's settings. This helps prevent intrusive ads and malicious pop-ups from interrupting your session.

Set Your Home Base

Set TeslaLink as your browser's homepage. This gives you immediate access to a curated collection of web apps every time you open the browser.

How do I add TeslaLink to my Tesla?

Simply open your Tesla's browser and navigate to TeslaLink. You can bookmark it for easy access later. No installation required!

Why aren't my apps syncing between devices?

App syncing uses your browser's localStorage. Make sure you're using the same browser and that you haven't cleared your browser data. The sync happens automatically when you make changes.

Can I suggest new apps to add?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new Tesla-compatible apps. Contact us through Ko-fi with your suggestions. We prioritize apps that work well on touchscreens and load quickly.

Is TeslaLink officially affiliated with Tesla?

No, TeslaLink is an independent project created by Tesla owners for Tesla owners. We are not affiliated with Tesla, Inc.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your app preferences and custom apps are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or store your personal app data on our servers. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.

What Tesla models are supported?

TeslaLink works on all Tesla models with web browser capability, including Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, and Cybertruck. The interface automatically adapts to different screen sizes.
