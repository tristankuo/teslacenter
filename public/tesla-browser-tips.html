üöÄ Performance & Speed Tips

‚ö°Clear Cache Regularly

Your Tesla browser's cache can become bloated over time, slowing down performance. Clear it regularly for optimal speed.

Step 1: Tap the menu button (three horizontal lines)
Step 2: Select "Settings" from the dropdown
Step 3: Find "Clear Browsing Data" or "Privacy & Security"
Step 4: Select cache, cookies, and browsing history
Step 5: Confirm and restart browser if needed

üì∂Optimize Your Connection

Use Wi-Fi whenever possible instead of cellular data. Tesla's Premium Connectivity provides unlimited data, but Wi-Fi is often faster and more stable.

  • Home: Connect to your home Wi-Fi before departure
  • Superchargers: Many locations offer free Wi-Fi
  • Hotels: Connect to hotel Wi-Fi for better streaming
  • Public Places: Use trusted public Wi-Fi networks

üîÑUse Multiple Tabs Wisely

Tesla browser supports multiple tabs, but too many can slow things down. Keep only essential tabs open and close unused ones.

  • Entertainment Tab: Keep one for YouTube/Netflix
  • Utility Tab: Weather, maps, or tools
  • TeslaCenter Tab: Quick access to your app collection
  • Close Others: Regularly close tabs you're not using
