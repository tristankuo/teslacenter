Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

TeslaCenter is a Tesla-optimized web application platform that helps organize and access Tesla-compatible apps. We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Local Storage Data: Your app preferences, custom apps, and theme settings are stored locally in your browser
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our platform (anonymous usage statistics)
  • Technical Data: Standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and access times

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the TeslaCenter platform
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage data for analytics purposes
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Most data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access
  • Data is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policies for Analytics and AdSense

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Google Analytics tracking
  • Google AdSense advertisement delivery
  • Storing your preferences locally

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • Google Analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Google Privacy Policy
  • External Apps: When you click on app links, you'll be subject to those apps' privacy policies

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored data by using browser settings
  • Opt out of analytics tracking using browser privacy settings
  • Request information about data we collect
  • Report privacy concerns to us

8. Children's Privacy

TeslaCenter is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Website: https://teslacenter.web.app
  • Support: https://ko-fi.com/teslacenter
