‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add TeslaCenter to my Tesla?

Simply open your Tesla's browser and navigate to teslacenter.web.app. You can bookmark it for easy access later. No installation required!

Why aren't my apps syncing between devices?

App syncing uses your browser's localStorage. Make sure you're using the same browser and that you haven't cleared your browser data. The sync happens automatically when you make changes.

Can I suggest new apps to add?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new Tesla-compatible apps. Contact us through Ko-fi with your suggestions. We prioritize apps that work well on touchscreens and load quickly.

Is TeslaCenter officially affiliated with Tesla?

No, TeslaCenter is an independent project created by Tesla owners for Tesla owners. We are not affiliated with Tesla, Inc.

Why do I see ads on TeslaCenter?

Ads help us keep TeslaCenter free for everyone while supporting ongoing development and hosting costs. We carefully select non-intrusive ads that won't interfere with your Tesla experience.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your app preferences and custom apps are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or store your personal app data on our servers. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.

What Tesla models are supported?

TeslaCenter works on all Tesla models with web browser capability, including Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, and Cybertruck. The interface automatically adapts to different screen sizes.
