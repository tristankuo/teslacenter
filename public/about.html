About TeslaCenter

TeslaCenter is the ultimate web platform designed for Tesla owners who want to get the most out of their in-vehicle browser. It's a curated, touch-optimized dashboard for all your favorite web apps.

What is TeslaCenter?

TeslaCenter transforms your car's browser into a powerful and easy-to-use app launcher. Whether you're charging, waiting, or have passengers looking for entertainment, TeslaCenter provides instant access to the best of the web, perfectly formatted for your Tesla's screen.

Core Features

Optimized for Tesla

A clean, fast interface with large, finger-friendly controls designed for the Tesla touchscreen.

Curated App Collection

We've handpicked the best web apps for entertainment, productivity, and travel that work seamlessly in your car.

Fully Customizable

Add your own favorite websites, arrange your dashboard, and sync your setup across devices.

Free & Private

TeslaCenter is completely free to use and respects your privacy. No installation or account is needed.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. Just open your Tesla's browser and go to teslacenter.web.app. From there, you can immediately start using the curated apps or customize the dashboard to fit your needs. Everything is saved locally in your browser for your convenience.

Community & Support

TeslaCenter is a community-driven project. We welcome your feedback and suggestions! For support or to suggest a new app, please visit our Contact Page. You can also support our development through Ko-fi.
